About Us

The People Behind The Jukebox Lounge NC

The Jukebox Lounge NC was created from a love of music, connection, culture, and unforgettable vibes. A space where people can relax, sing along, dance, connect, and feel good.

Meet the Owner

Keeva Nichols

Hey, I’m Keeva — the founder and visionary behind The Jukebox Lounge NC.

What started as an idea turned into a passion project built around good music, good energy, and bringing people back together again. I created The Jukebox Lounge NC because I missed the feeling of real vibes — the kind of nights where people actually connect, sing along to the music, dance, laugh, unwind, and just feel good.

Music has always created memories for me. From old-school R&B and soulful classics to grown & sexy lounge vibes, I wanted to build experiences that feel nostalgic, welcoming, and authentic. Not just another event, but a feeling people remember.

The Jukebox Lounge NC is more than parties or pop-ups to me. It’s the beginning of a bigger vision — creating a space and brand centered around culture, community, creativity, and experiences that bring people together.

Whether it’s a live music night, a DJ battle, a rooftop vibe, or an intimate grown folks experience, my goal is always the same: create moments people enjoy, remember, and want to come back to.

Thank you to everyone supporting the vision early. Every ticket purchase, membership, repost, and word-of-mouth recommendation truly means more than you know.
